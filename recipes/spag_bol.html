Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

This page has the recipe for cooking Spaghetti Bolognese, which is a pasta dish with beef mince in tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 250g Spaghetti
  • 500g beef mince
  • Can of chopped tomatoes
  • One chopped onion
  • Garlic
  • Italian herbs

Steps

  1. Add the garlic to a hot frying pan for one minute, then add the mince and brown
  2. Once browned, add the italian herbs and onion to the frying pan for a few minutes, followed by the chopped tomatoes
  3. Once bubbling reduce the heat to a simmer
  4. Cook the spaghetti as per instructions (put on some garlic bread if it takes your fancy)
  5. Once the pasta is cooked take the Bolognese sauce off the heat and dish up
